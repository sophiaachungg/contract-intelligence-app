CONTRACT ID: ERG-CORP-2024-023
TYPE: Professional Services Agreement – Strategy and Operations Consulting
BRANCH: Corporate (Ergon, Inc.)
PARTIES: Ergon, Inc. ("Company") | Northbridge Advisory Group, LLC ("Consultant")
EFFECTIVE DATE: June 10, 2024
EXPIRATION DATE: June 9, 2025
AUTO-RENEWAL: No

RECITALS
Company engages Consultant to provide strategy, process improvement, and project management support for internal operational initiatives.

1. SERVICES
Consultant shall provide advisory services, workshop facilitation, process mapping, and project tracking support as described in individual statements of work. Consultant shall not make decisions on behalf of Company.

2. FEES
Consultant shall be paid at the rates set forth in Exhibit A. Invoices shall be issued monthly and payable net 30. Approved travel expenses are reimbursable at cost.

3. WORK PRODUCT
All deliverables specifically created for Company and paid for in full shall be owned by Company. Consultant retains ownership of its pre-existing templates, frameworks, and know-how.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Consultant shall keep Company information confidential during the Term and for 5 years thereafter. Consultant shall limit access to Company information to personnel with a need to know.

5. NON-SOLICITATION
During the Term and for 12 months thereafter, Consultant shall not solicit Company employees with whom Consultant had direct contact in the prior 12 months.

**RISK FLAG - BROAD NON-SOLICIT:** The clause is limited to employees, but the surrounding language around "direct contact" is vague. In practice, disputes may arise over which personnel interactions count as solicitation activity.

6. INDEMNIFICATION
Consultant shall indemnify Company for third-party claims arising from Consultant's negligence or willful misconduct. Company shall indemnify Consultant for claims resulting from Company-provided materials or instructions.

7. LIABILITY CAP
Consultant's total liability shall not exceed the fees paid under the applicable statement of work in the 6 months preceding the claim.

**RISK FLAG - TIGHT LIABILITY WINDOW:** Tying liability to only the prior 6 months of fees can substantially reduce recoverable damages in longer projects, especially if a claim surfaces after work has already ended.

8. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate for convenience with 30 days written notice. Company shall pay for all work performed through the termination date plus any non-cancelable commitments approved in writing.

GOVERNING LAW: State of Mississippi
