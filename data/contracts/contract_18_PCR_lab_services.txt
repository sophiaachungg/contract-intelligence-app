CONTRACT ID: ERG-PCR-2024-018
TYPE: Laboratory Services Agreement – Asphalt and Emulsion Testing
BRANCH: PCR (Pavement & Coating Resources)
PARTIES: Ergon Pavement & Coating Resources, LLC ("Laboratory") | Great Plains Asphalt, LLC ("Client")
EFFECTIVE DATE: February 1, 2024
EXPIRATION DATE: January 31, 2026
AUTO-RENEWAL: Yes, annual, 30-day written notice required to terminate

RECITALS
Laboratory provides analytical and performance testing services for asphalt emulsions, polymer modified binders, and related paving materials. Client submits samples for quality verification, formulation screening, and field-performance evaluation.

1. SERVICES
Laboratory shall perform the test methods identified in each purchase order or statement of work, including but not limited to viscosity, residue, storage stability, and wheel-tracking tests. Laboratory shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet requested turnaround times.

2. FEES
Standard testing fees are set forth in Exhibit A. Expedited testing within 3 business days requires a 40% rush surcharge. Payment terms are net 30. Overdue amounts accrue interest at 1.25% per month.

3. SAMPLE RETENTION
Laboratory may retain a representative retained sample for up to 45 days after delivery of results unless Client requests earlier destruction in writing.

4. REPORTING
Laboratory shall deliver a written report for each completed sample set. Reports are intended for informational purposes only and do not constitute a warranty that the tested material will perform in all field conditions.

**RISK FLAG - RELIANCE DISCLAIMER:** The report disclaimer limits Client's ability to assert that it relied on the laboratory result as a performance guarantee. This can be problematic where the testing outcome is used to release large production batches.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Laboratory shall not disclose Client's formulations, specifications, or test results except to its employees and contractors with a need to know or as required by law. Laboratory shall provide prompt notice of any compelled disclosure unless prohibited by law.

6. LIABILITY LIMITATION
Laboratory's liability for erroneous testing shall be limited to the lesser of (i) the amount paid for the test giving rise to the claim or (ii) re-performance of the test at no charge.

**RISK FLAG - EXTREMELY LOW REMEDY:** If an incorrect result causes significant downstream losses, the remedy is still limited to the test fee or a free retest, which is unusually narrow for a quality-control service.

7. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate for convenience with 30 days written notice. Client remains responsible for completed work and any non-cancelable specialty materials.

GOVERNING LAW: State of Mississippi
