CONTRACT ID: ERG-PCR-2024-019
TYPE: Field Trial and Product Evaluation Agreement
BRANCH: PCR (Pavement & Coating Resources)
PARTIES: Ergon Pavement & Coating Resources, LLC ("Developer") | Southeastern Transportation Authority ("Authority")
EFFECTIVE DATE: August 15, 2024
EXPIRATION DATE: August 14, 2025
AUTO-RENEWAL: No

RECITALS
Developer is engaged in the development of asphalt and coating technologies. Authority seeks to evaluate a new polymer modified pavement system on designated roadway segments under controlled field conditions.

1. PILOT PROGRAM
Developer shall provide product formulation support, application guidance, and on-site technical observation during the field trial. Authority shall designate test sections, coordinate contractor access, and supervise traffic control.

2. PROJECT FUNDING
Authority shall pay a fixed project fee of $214,000. Fifty percent shall be due upon mobilization and the remainder upon delivery of the final evaluation memorandum.

3. PERFORMANCE DATA
Authority shall furnish traffic counts, weather logs, and maintenance records reasonably requested by Developer for analysis. Developer may use de-identified trial data for internal product improvement purposes.

4. OWNERSHIP
All project-specific reports and technical notes created by Developer for Authority shall be owned by Authority after full payment. Developer retains ownership of pre-existing methods, models, and templates.

**RISK FLAG - DATA RIGHTS AMBIGUITY:** The clause allows Developer to use de-identified trial data but does not define what constitutes de-identification. If the dataset includes location-specific roadway data, re-identification risk may remain.

5. WARRANTY AND DISCLAIMER
Developer does not warrant that the pilot product will achieve any specific service life, rut resistance, or crack resistance beyond the conditions expressly set forth in Exhibit A.

6. LIABILITY
Developer's liability for project-related claims shall not exceed the total amount paid under the Agreement. Developer shall not be responsible for failures caused by subgrade conditions, contractor deviation, or unauthorized material substitution.

**RISK FLAG - FIELD FAILURE ALLOCATION:** The Agreement places most of the project failure risk on Authority but does not define a technical dispute process for separating formulation failure from contractor installation error.

7. TERMINATION
Authority may terminate for convenience with 45 days written notice. Developer shall be paid for completed milestones and approved travel expenses incurred before termination.

GOVERNING LAW: State of Mississippi
